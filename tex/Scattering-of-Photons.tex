\chapter{Scattering of Photons}
\section{Scattering of Photons}

In the scattering of photons, for example from an atom, an initial state photon with wave-number $\vec{k}$ and polarization $\displaystyle \hat{\epsilon}$ is absorbed by the atom and a final state photon with wave-number $\vec{k}'$ and polarization $\displaystyle \hat{\epsilon}'$ is emitted.
